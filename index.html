THP averages 134 bar — 3.5x higher than any other well. This well appears to be in a fundamentally different pressure regime, possibly naturally flowing at high reservoir pressure rather than requiring conventional gas lift.
🟡
Well_312E — Zero TAP Throughout
TAP = 0 bar for all readings. No tubing annulus pressure detected. This may indicate a different completion architecture, a closed annulus valve, or a sensor failure at this measurement point.
🟡
Well_206B & Well_209B — Identical Data
Both wells share exactly the same THP, CHP, TAP and water cut readings across all 923 rows. These may be the same physical well under two IDs, or data was duplicated in the field collection system.

Well_296A — Deliberate A/B Configuration Study
Well_296A appears in both Standard GL and Long String GL datasets with identical readings. This is a planned comparison study — not an error — designed to evaluate whether a long string completion improves lift performance.
